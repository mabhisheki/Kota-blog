KOTA: The E-motion of Rajasthan

Kota is a city on the Chambal river in Rajasthan,India.Kota is the heart of Rajasthan. Kota is not only a place,it is an emotion for Engineering/Medical aspirants.

Life in Kota was tough. A life of full of memories. Memories which You will cherish for a subsequent amount of time.Kota taught you alot of things like how to blance yourself in any tough situation,selection of best or worst option,how much pressure you can bear and no need to talk about the philosphical stuff again and again.

But there was one particular memory which had a profound impact on me and I can never forget the importance of that specific gracious time.What is the sense of learning periodic table of 118 member,when eventually we will not able to remember.

Leaving home at a tender age of 17 was a big issue for my family.Because it was first time when I was so far to them.I was all pampered by my peers as if I am going on a war(which I think i really was).

During my initial days in the Mecca of JEE,I met some guys.They are from Bihar,Jharkhand,Delhi,UP and RAjasthan.They were full of life.They always had a smile on their face,greeted me everytime we aquainteed.Slowly,our companionship grew.I sat beside them everyday in the lectures,impressed with the fact that they knew a lot of things beforehand.They were an ingenious guy.I was glad ,I had a good company.

One of them, on one fine day,I observed that he had been skipping his afternoon meals.On asking the reason he exclaimed that he was on a financial crunch.Fine I guess ..a lot of used to do that.A few days letter,once we visited a park just to hang out which was where he told me about his life.

He was in Kota since 8th standard,joining the foundation course in a well known institute(it's like pre JEE coaching.He remarked that he came to Kotaon his own,witha suitcase,some cash and a hope to make it big one day.His parents did not drop him off the first time he came to Kota.He found out some paying guest house on his own and started his journey in 8th standard.He had spent 4 years in this kingdom of competitiveness.In these 4 years his parents never visited him and he went home once or twice due to some familial issues he remarked.

After a while it was getting dark,I knew my mess would be closed by now, so he invited me to his roomto share his tiffin.As soon as I entered the room,I was shocked,flagbbergasted,stunned.His room was smaller than my store room.A cupboard,a table that's it.I still remember the photographic moment,with a tear rolling down me eye. why? why? why did he have to endure so much?It's just human nature to compare..I thought about my life in retrospect.I had...!
